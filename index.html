About me

Hi! I am Marjolein and I am a front-end developer based in Utrecht, the Netherlands.

I enjoy building websites and apps that are responsive, accessible and interactive. What I like about development is that I can combine my creativity, solution focused mentality and my eagerness to learn into beautiful web pages and apps. I enjoy the satisfaction of seeing clear results as well as the process of getting there. Before learning web development I wore many different hats, though always focusing on improving people’s (work) lives, coming up with creative solutions and making memorable content. When I am not coding, you can find me singing in a choir, trying out new recipes or working as a volunteer in a cute feminist bookshop. I can’t wait to work with you!
